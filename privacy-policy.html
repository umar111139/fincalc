✅ Short version: FinCalc does not collect, store, or sell any personal information. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We never see your numbers.

📥 1. Information We Collect

FinCalc is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • The numbers you enter into our calculators
  • Your financial data, salary, loan amounts, or investment figures
  • Account information (we have no accounts or login system)

The only data stored on your device is your currency preference, saved in your browser's localStorage so it persists between visits. This data never leaves your device.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data through Google Analytics (if enabled), such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General geographic region (country level only)
  • Device type and browser (e.g., mobile/desktop, Chrome/Safari)
  • Referring website

This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

⚙️ 2. How We Use Information

Anonymous usage data, if collected, is used solely to:

  • Understand which calculators are most used
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Make decisions about new features

We never sell, rent, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

📢 3. Google AdSense & Cookies

FinCalc is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to FinCalc and other websites.

ℹ️ Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Types of cookies that may be set:

  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics to measure traffic (anonymous)
  • Functional cookies — set by FinCalc to remember your currency preference (localStorage, not a cookie)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the display of ads but will not affect calculator functionality.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

🔗 4. Third-Party Links

FinCalc may contain links to external websites for reference or additional information. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit. Our Privacy Policy applies only to FinCalc (fincalc.com).

🛡️ 5. Data Storage & Security

Since FinCalc does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to secure on our servers. All calculator computations happen entirely in your browser using JavaScript — your numbers never leave your device.

The only data stored locally is your currency preference in browser localStorage. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

👦 6. Children's Privacy

FinCalc is a general-purpose financial tools website intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through our website, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

✊ 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding personal data, including:

  • The right to access any data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to data portability (GDPR)

Since we collect no personal data, there is typically nothing to access or delete. For advertising-related rights, please use Google's tools directly.

📝 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we update the policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of FinCalc after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
