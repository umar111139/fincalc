🎯 3. Accuracy of Calculations

FinCalc uses standard financial formulas widely accepted in the industry. However, actual results from banks, lenders, and financial institutions may differ due to:

  • Processing fees, origination fees, and other charges not included in basic EMI calculations
  • Variable interest rate changes over the loan term
  • Lender-specific rounding rules and payment schedules
  • Prepayment penalties or rewards
  • Changes in tax laws, insurance premiums, or regulatory requirements
  • Actual market returns differing from assumed rates in SIP and compound interest calculators
⚠️ Always verify: Use FinCalc results as an estimate and cross-check with your actual lender, bank, or financial institution before signing any agreement.
